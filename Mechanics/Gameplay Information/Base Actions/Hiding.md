## Hiding Overview
You may attempt to hide from non-allied creatures to avoid their detection. Hiding takes one action and you must make a Dexterity (Stealth) check. The DC for this check is 10 + the highest [[Sight-Based Perception]] modifier among the non-allied creatures that can see you. The Dexterity check has

A modifier more than or equal to +5 is an automatic success
