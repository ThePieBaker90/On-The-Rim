## Critical Overview
- There are two types of criticals, critical successes and critical failures. Criticals naturally occur when a 20 sided dice rolls a 20 or 1 and they allow for extraordinary feats and surprising downfalls.
## Critical Successes
- A critical success occurs when the result of a d20 roll with no modifiers is equal to or greater than your critical success threshold.
- The base critical success threshold is 20.
- When a critical success occurs on a skill check or saving throw, it automatically succeeds. This usually causes easier or automatic successes on subsequent checks. This is highly context dependent.
- When a critical success occurs on a roll to hit, it is called a critical hit. The attack automatically hits, doubles all damage of its hit effects, and the attacker rolls on the corresponding [[#Critical Effect Tables|Critical Effect Table]].
## Critical Failures
- A critical failure occurs when the result of a d20 roll with no modifiers is equal to or lesser than your critical failure threshold.
- The base critical failure threshold is 1.
- When a critical failure occurs on a skill check or saving throw, it automatically fails, usually causing harder or automatic failures on subsequent checks. This is highly context dependent.
- When a critical failure occurs on a roll to hit, it is called a critical miss. The attack automatically misses. 
	- If the weapon used is a firearm it gains [[Jammed]].
	- If the weapon used is a non-firearm weapon it hits a random allied creature that a miss could reasonably hit.
## Critical Effect Tables
- You roll on a critical effect table when you make a critical hit. The critical effect table you roll on is determined by the leftmost damage type dealt by an attack's hit effects. If an attack does not deal damage on its hit effects, it does not roll on a hit effect table.
	- Bludgeoning damage rolls on the [[Bludgeoning Critical Effect Table]].
	- Cold damage rolls on the [[Cold Critical Effect Table]].
	- Corrosion damage rolls on the [[Corrosion Critical Effect Table]].
	- Electric damage rolls on the [[Electric Critical Effect Table]].
	- Fire damage rolls on the [[Fire Critical Effect Table]].
	- Force damage rolls on the [[Force Critical Effect Table]].
	- Necrotic damage rolls on the [[Necrotic Critical Effect Table]].
	- Piercing damage rolls on the [[Piercing Critical Effect Table]].
	- Poison damage rolls on the [[Poison Critical Effect Table]].
	- Psychic damage rolls on the [[Psychic Critical Effect Table]].
	- Radiant damage rolls on the [[Radiant Critical Effect Table]].
	- Slashing damage rolls on the [[Slashing Critical Effect Table]].
	- Sonic damage rolls on the [[Sonic Critical Effect Table]].
