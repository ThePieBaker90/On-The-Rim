## **Assaulter**
**Stat changes**
* Med. increase to con, str, hp, small point buy
* 
**Abilities**
* Adrenaline boost
  * Consumable
  * Default 3 maximum capacity (increases at higher levels)
  * Replenish fully on long rest
  * Replenishes 1 per 10 hp lost (doesn't include temp hp)
  * Use: bonus action
  * Effect lasts 18s
  * Grants resistance to all damage types except necrotic and psychic
  * Grants advantage to all str and con saves and checks
  * At higher levels, grants additional bonus action
  * At even higher levels, grants additional main action (instead of bonus)
* Draw Fire
  * Free action to start/stop
  * Grants advantage on attacks against self
  * Grants disadvantage on attacks against allies within 10ft or behind you
* Fire and Fury
  * When making an attack at a range of 15ft or less, target must make a DC 10+level con save or become frightened of you for 1 turn
* Last Stand (high level ability?)
  * When reduced to 0hp, automatically use an adrenaline shot, and got to 1hp. Hp cannot decrease below 1 while adrenaline boost effect is active. When the adrenaline boost runs out, drop to 0 hp.
