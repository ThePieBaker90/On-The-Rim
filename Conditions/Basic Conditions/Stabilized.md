- When a creature has the stabilized condition they suffer the following effects:
	- They are [[Incapacitated]],
	- They are [[Prone]] and cannot remove this condition by getting up.
	- A help action can be used to regenerate this creature to 1 health and remove this condition
	- If they receive damage, they immediately gain the [[Dying]] condition and remove this condition.
