- Organ decay may only affect [[Humanoid Body|Body Parts]] that are internal and natural.
- When a [[Humanoid Body|Body Part]] has the organ decay condition, it suffers the following effects:
	- The part has 50% of its base efficiency.
	- The part will be destroyed 2d20 hours after this condition is gained.
		- The amount of time is rolled when the condition is gained.
