- Armor corrosion always has an indefinite duration.
- Armor corrosion may stack.
- Armor corrosion may only affect armor.
- When a piece of armor has one or more levels of armor corrosion, it suffers the following effects:
	- Each level of armor corrosion reduces the armor's effective [[Armor Class]] by 1.
		- This cannot reduce the armor class of a piece of armor below 0.
	- 10 minutes of work on the armor with 5% of the armor's cost worth of materials will remove one level of this condition from the armor.
