- Anger always has an indefinite duration.
- Anger may stack.
- When a creature has one or more levels of anger, they suffer the following effects:
	- They make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw at the beginning of their turn. On a success they lose 1 level of anger.
		- They automatically fail this saving throw if they gained a level of anger after the start of their last turn.
		- This saving throw does not trigger effects that would grant one or more levels of anger.
	- They suffer the effects listed in the following table:

| Anger Level | Damage Modifiers                                         | Hit Modifiers                       | Charisma Effects                                                       | Other Effects                                                  |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1           | They deal 1d4 additional damage with all melee attacks.  | They have -1 to hit on all attacks. | They have disadvantage on all non-intimidation charisma checks.        |                                                                |
| 2           | They deal 1d6 additional damage with all melee attacks.  | They have -2 to hit on all attacks. | They have disadvantage and -1 on all non-intimidation charisma checks. |                                                                |
| 3           | They deal 1d8 additional damage with all melee attacks.  | They have -3 to hit on all attacks. | They have disadvantage and -2 on all non-intimidation charisma checks. |                                                                |
| 4           | They deal 1d10 additional damage with all melee attacks. | They have -4 to hit on all attacks. | They automatically fail all non-intimidation charisma checks.          | They cannot concentrate on psycasts.                           |
| 5+          | They deal 1d12 additional damage with all melee attacks. | They have -5 to hit on all attacks. | They automatically fail all non-intimidation charisma checks.          | They gain [[Berserk Rage]] until their anger level is below 5. |
- The additional damage has the type of the highest damage amount of the base attack.
- A creature with this condition is immune to [[Charmed]], [[Emotional Guidance]], [[Joy]], and [[Panic]].
- Constructs and creatures who do not feel emotion are immune to this condition.
