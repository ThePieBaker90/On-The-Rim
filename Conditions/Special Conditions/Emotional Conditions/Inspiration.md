- Inspiration may stack.
- When a creature has one or more levels of inspiration, they may reroll 1 dice of choice at anytime during the condition. The outcome of this reroll replaces the result of the dice.
	- Using this ability removes 1 level of inspiration.
	- They do not need to be the one to roll the dice to use this ability.
- Constructs and creatures who do not feel emotion are immune to this condition.
