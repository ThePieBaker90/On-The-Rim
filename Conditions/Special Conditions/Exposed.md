- When a creature has the Exposed condition, they suffer the following effects:
	- They take an extra 1d4 radiant damage from all damage.
	- They automatically fail all stealth checks. 
	- They give out a bright light which extends 5 ft away in every direction.
- A creature who is immune to radiant damage is immune to this condition.
