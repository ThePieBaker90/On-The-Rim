*Frostlords specialize in bending water and ice to their will. Most of their psycasts center around cold and piercing damage, with many emulating firearms. Many effects also apply [[Frozen Solid]], which can temporarily take a creature out of a fight.*
## Core Psycasts
- 1st Level:
	- [[Fragment Burst]] (2, 23)
	- [[Hailshot]] (2, 20)
- 2nd Level:
	- [[Freeze Ray]] (4, 22)
	- [[Cooling Crystal]] (24, 0)
- 3rd Level:
	- [[Ice Wall]] (6, 28)
	- [[Cryo-Freeze]] (14, 15)
	- [[Snap Freeze]] (4, 37)
- 4th Level:
	- [[Arctic Wind]] (3, 44)
- 5th Level:
	- [[Icicle Impale]] (5, 50)
	- [[Temperature Plunge]] (30, 38)
## Miscasts
- Level 1 Miscast - Sudden Ice Rink
	- Ice spreads around you covering the nearby floors. All surfaces within a sphere of 20ft radius centered on you cost an additional 1ft of [[Movement Speed]] per 1ft used to traverse the ice. This ice lasts for 3 turns.
- Level 2 Miscast - Self Popsicle
	- You become encased in a solid block of ice. You gain the [[Frozen Solid]] condition for 3 turns.
