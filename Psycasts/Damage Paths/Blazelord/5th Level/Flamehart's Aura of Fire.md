- **Path**:
	- 5th Level [[Blazelord Psycasts|Blazelord Psycast]]
- **Casting Time:**
	- 1 Action.
- **[[Psyfocus]] Cost:**
	- 12
- **[[Neural Heat]] Generation:**
	- 58 (43)
- [[Range]]:
	- Self.
- **Target**:
	- A sphere with its center within range.
- **Components**:
	- Gesture
- **Duration**:
	- Concentration, up to 5 turns.
- **Effects**:
	- **
	- The target sphere is centered on you at all times. 
	- While a creature is in the target sphere, they are afflicted.
	- Afflicted creatures suffer the following effects:
		- They take damage from the heat at the end of their turn.
		- They catch [[On Fire!]] at the end of their turn.
			- If they already have [[On Fire!]], the duration of it increases instead of decreasing.
	- The damage from this psycast is not increased by [[Burned]].
	- You are immune to the damaging and condition effects of this psycast.
- **Stats**:
	- 1d4 + 2 Fire Damage from Heat.
	- 1 Turn [[On Fire!]] Duration.
	- +1 Turn of [[On Fire!]] on Repeat Application.
	- 5 Turn [[On Fire!]] Limit.
	- 60ft Target Sphere Radius.
- **Upcasting**:
	- You cannot upcast this psycast.
