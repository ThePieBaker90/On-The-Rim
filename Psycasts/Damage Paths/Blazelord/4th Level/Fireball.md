- **Path**:
	- 4th Level [[Blazelord Psycasts|Blazelord Psycast]]
- **Casting Time:**
	- 1 Action.
- **[[Psyfocus]] Cost:**
	- 3
- **[[Neural Heat]] Generation:**
	- 44
- [[Range]]:
	- 40ft.
- **Target**:
	- A sphere with its center within range, may go over obstacles.
- **Components**:
	- Gesture.
- **Duration**:
	- Instant.
- **Effects**:
	- *You lob a small ball of fire, the ball grows as it flies through the air eventually landing and creating a massive fireball.*
	- All creatures within the target sphere must make a Dexterity saving throw to dodge the fireball. 
		- On a failure, they take heavy damage and are set [[On Fire!]].
		- On a success, they take half damage.
	- All flammables in the target sphere will catch on fire.
- **Stats**:
	- 4d8 Fire Damage from the Fireball.
	- 2 Turn [[On Fire!]] Duration.
	- 15ft Target Sphere Radius. (+5ft)
- **Upcasting**:
	- You may upcast this psycast. For every 17 psyfocus spent above the normal cost for this psycast, the radius of the target sphere increases by 5ft.
