- **Path**:
	- 2nd Level [[Staticlord Psycasts|Staticlord Psycast]]
- **Casting Time:**
	- 1 Action.
- **[[Psyfocus]] Cost:**
	- 10
- **[[Neural Heat]] Generation:**
	- 26
- [[Range]]:
	- 35ft.
- **Target**:
	- A sphere with its center within range.
- **Components**:
	- Kinetic.
- **Duration**:
	- Instant.
- **Effects**:
	- *You create a large electromagnetic pulse at an area disabling all electronics in the area for a short time.*
	- Within the target sphere, the following effects occur:
		- All non-[[Cybernetics|Cybernetic]] or non-EMP-shielded electronics are disabled for 3 turns. 
		- Each creature with a [[Cybernetics|Cybernetic]] must make a constitution saving throw.
			- On a failure, all [[Cybernetics]] the creature has are [[Conditions/Special Conditions/EMP Stunned|EMP Stunned]].
			- On a success, nothing happens.
		- Each construct must make a constitution saving throw. 
			- On a failure, the creature is [[Conditions/Special Conditions/EMP Stunned|EMP Stunned]].
			- On a success, nothing happens.
- **Stats**:
	- 3 Turn Electronic Disable Duration.
	- 2 Turn [[Cybernetics|Cybernetic]] [[Conditions/Special Conditions/EMP Stunned|EMP Stunned]] Duration.
	- 2 Turn Construct [[Conditions/Special Conditions/EMP Stunned|EMP Stunned]] Duration.
	- 15ft Target Sphere Radius. (+5ft)
- **Upcasting**:
	- You may upcast this psycast. For every 20 psyfocus spent above the normal cost for this psycast, the radius of the target sphere increases by 5ft.
