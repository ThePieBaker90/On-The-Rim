*Toxilords specialize in infections, radiation, and poisons. Most of their psycasts center around poison damage and damage over time effects. [[Poison Buildup]] is common in their psycasts, dealing increasing damage over time.*
## Core Psycasts
- 1st Level:
	- [[Poison Dart]] (2, 15)
- 2nd Level:
	- [[Toxic Trap]] (22, 0)
	- [[Contaminate]] (22, 0)
	- [[Filter Poisons]] (12, 24)
- 3rd Level:
	- [[Poison Spray]] (5, 37)
	- [[Radioactivate]] (7, 28)
- 4th Level:
	- [[Poison Cloud]] (3, 44)
	- [[Infect]] (4, 27)
- 5th Level:
	- [[Excite Toxins]] (14, 56)
	- [[Toxin Duplication]] (12, 58)
## Miscasts
- Level 1 Miscast - Toxin Overload
	- Natural toxins present in your body grow in strength, you gain 5 levels of [[Poison Buildup]].
- Level 2 Miscast - Poison Dissipate
	- All levels of [[Poison Buildup]] you have applied to creatures in the last 3 rounds are lost. Creatures take 1 poison damage per level removed.
