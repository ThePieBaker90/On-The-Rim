- **Path**:
	- 2nd Level [[Mortilord Psycasts|Mortilord Psycast]]
- **Casting Time:**
	- 1 Bonus Action
- **[[Psyfocus]] Cost:**
	- 6
- **[[Neural Heat]] Generation:**
	- 23
- [[Range]]:
	- 40ft
- **Target**:
	- A creature within range.
- **Components**:
	- Verbal
- **Duration**:
	- Instant.
- **Effects**
	- You shout a word of harming, target creature within range must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failure, they take 2d4 necrotic damage and gain [[Antiheal]] for 1 turn. On a success, nothing happens.
- **Upcasting**:
	- You may upcast this psycast. For every 18 psyfocus spent above the normal cost for this psycast, this psycast deals an additional 1d10 necrotic damage.
