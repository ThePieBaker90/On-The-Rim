*Skipmancers specialize in skipping, a form of teleportation. Most of their psycasts teleport an object or creature to a chosen point or a random point. They are masters at manipulating positions on the battlefield.*

## Miscasts
- Level 1 Miscast - Unwilling Chaos Skip
	- You and everything that you have equipped and stored on you teleports to a random grounded point within 50ft. You do not need line of sight to the point.
- Level 2 Miscast - Battle Reorganize
	- All creatures within 100ft of you teleport to a random point within 100ft of you. You do not need line of sight to the creatures or the random point. All equipped and stored items teleport with the creature.
