- **Path**:
	- 4th level [[Aquator Psycasts|Aquator Psycast]]
- **Casting Time**:
	- 1 Minute.
- **[[Psyfocus]] Cost:**
	- 26
- **[[Neural Heat]] Generation:**
	- 0
- [[Range]]:
	- 50ft.
- **Target**:
	- Food or drink within range.
- **Components**:
	- Kinetic.
- **Duration**:
	- Instant.
- **Effects**:
	- *Ambient moisture in the air draws toxins out of a liquid through diffusion.*
	- You purify one volume of liquid (up to 15L) or one piece of food (up to 3kg) from any poisons, contaminants, or diseases. 
		- Certain contaminants cannot be removed by this psycast.
- **Upcasting:**
	- You may upcast this psycast. For every 8 psyfocus spent above the normal cost for this psycast, you may purify 15L more worth of liquid or 3kg more worth of food.
