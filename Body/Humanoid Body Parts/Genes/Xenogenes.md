## Xenogene Overview
- Xenogenes are a form of [[Genes|Gene]] and the building blocks for [[Xenotypes]]. They list the unique effects of a [[Xenotypes|Xenotype]] and act like traits.
- If a xenogene were to conflict with an [[Endogenes|Endogene]], the xenogene has priority.
