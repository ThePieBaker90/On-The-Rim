## Overview
Lower-Body Strength and Dexterity are based on Strength and Dexterity respectively.
****
## Lower-Body Strength
Determines the following:
- Athletics (Leg Based)
- Unnarmed Attacks Melee Attacks with the Legs
- Carrying Capacity
****
## Lower-Body Dexterity
Determines the following:
- Stealth
- Acrobatics
- Armor Class
- Initiative
- Hiding
