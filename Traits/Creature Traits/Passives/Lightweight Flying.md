- A creature with the Lightweight Flying trait has 40 ft of flight speed as long as they are not wearing heavy armor or medium armor.

- **[[Metabolic Efficiency]]:**
	- -2
- **Effects:**
	- Carriers of this gene may fly and have 40ft of flying speed. 
	- This gene is disabled while the carrier is wearing heavy armor, medium armor, or a [[Warcasket]].
