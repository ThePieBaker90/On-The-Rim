- **[[Metabolic Efficiency]]:**
	- -1
- **Effects:**
	- Carriers of this gene gain the Toxbite action. They may do this action at the cost of 200ml of stored [[Materials/Blood|Blood]].
		- Toxbite - Action
			- You attempt to bite and inject venom into a nearby creature. Make a melee roll to hit against the creature. On a hit, you deal 1d4 + DEX piercing damage, 1d6 poison damage and they gain 3 levels of [[Radiation Buildup]].
