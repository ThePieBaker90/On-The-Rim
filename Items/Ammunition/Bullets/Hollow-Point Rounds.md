- **Usable In:**
	- Assault Rifles, Submachine Guns, Pistols, and Revolvers.
- **Effect:**
	- Additional damage depending on the [[Rate of Fire]] of the weapon
		- +1 piercing damage if ROF >= 4
		- +1d4 piercing damage if ROF >= 2
		- +1d6 piercing damage if ROF < 2
	- -2 to hit.
- **Cost:**
	- 1 credit for 10
