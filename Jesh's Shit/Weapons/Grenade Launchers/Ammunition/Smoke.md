- Aimed at a target or point within range
- Minimum range 10ft
- Creates 15ft cube of thick white smoke that lasts 1 minute unless dispersed by eg. wind
- Smoke blocks line-of-sight
- Thermal, sonic, or magic-based sight is unaffected
