* [[Range]]:
	* Ranged: 30-75-110ft
* __Damage__:
  * All creatures within 15ft radius and line-of-sight of target must make a DC12 Dex saving throw.
    * On a failure, they take 2d6 heat damage and gain On Fire! for three turns
    * On a success, they take half 2d6 heat damage
    * The target makes this save with disadvantage
* [[Rate of Fire]]:
	* 1

* **Properties**:

	* Heavy
  * Single-Use
  * Special
* **Special**
  * Backblast
    * All creatures within line-of-sight and in a 20ft long, 5ft wide cone directly behind the user take 1d6 heat damage
    * All creatures within line-of-sight and in a 10ft long, 5ft wide cone directly behind the user, in addition to above effects, take 1d6 force damage and are knocked prone
    * All creatures within line-of-sight and in a 5ft long, 5ft wide cone directly behind the user, in addition to above effects, take 1d6 force damage and are knocked back 5ft

The M57A6 "Brimstone" version is features an enlarged warhead packed with a sticky chemfuel-based incendiary mixture, ignited and dispersed by a small conventional explosive.
