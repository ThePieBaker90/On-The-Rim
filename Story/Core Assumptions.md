* The world does not have magic, although there is technology indistinguishable from magic. One of these sufficiently advanced technologies is [[NOR-1 My First Psylink|Psylinks]]. [[NOR-1 My First Psylink|Psylinks]] give people the ability to use psycasts and gain class benefits. They level up as they become more attuned with your brain, allowing for more possibilities! [[NOR-1 My First Psylink|Psylinks]] are created by an [[The Archotech|Archotech]] but may be modified by anyone who knows what they are doing
* The world suffered a great disaster 1000 years ago, referred to as the calamity, where it was suddenly destroyed and with it went 99.9% of all sentient life. The survivors who lived on exoplanets or were out in space were left to pick up the pieces of a shattered race. 
	* Without support from earth and because of planetary struggles, many humans evolved to better deal with the harsh conditions. These human variants are known as [[Germlines]].
		* Galaxy population levels have since far exceeded pre-calamity population levels.
	* After the calamity, many formed factions and corporations, attempting to be the new leader of mankind. 
		* The most successful of these factions is known as the empire, a space monarchy with the prime directive to spread its peace, whether it be through words or force.
	* Technological growth post-calamity was stunted briefly before being picked back up by major factions and corporations. Technology growth from humans eventually slowed down, as more and more of it was done by [[The Archotech|Archotechs]], the last true innovation.
		* One of the notable innovations is faster than light (FTL) travel for space ships.
		* Another notable innovation is cryogenics. Cryogenics have progressed to the point that creatures can be put into a suspended animation where they don’t age and they can later be taken out of with no permanent adverse effects.
		* Not all factions are at the same technology levels, some factions never recovered beyond pre-calamity technology.

* Universal time is (like computer time, but not earths time cuz it gone).Local time is dependent on the planet, this time usually begins when the planet was discovered.
* All factions have varying technology levels, although most tend to be high-tech. Additionally, all factions have very different styles of architecture and cultures.
* Guns exist and are prominent, but that does not mean melee is useless, [[Items/Armor/Properties/Shield Belt|Shield Belts]] and [[Items/Accessories/Belts/Jump Pack|Jump Packs]] allow melee users to soak up damage and close the gap between them and an opponent respectively. Additionally, certain guns may be extremely difficult to use at close range (such as a [[Sniper Rifle]]) or ineffective at long range (such as a [[Submachine Gun]])
* Gods and Goddesses are created by their believers and gain powers from their believers. Every religion has an ideology which can be fluid or rigid. When all followers of an ideology are killed or converted the god ceases to exist. Not every ideology has a deity. Some ideologies have holy relics that are sought after for their ideological significance or technological significance. All ideologies have memes which are described in the memes section TODO.
* New Proficiencies: Pistols, Revolvers, Anti-Material Rifles, Assault Rifles, Submachine Guns, Sniper Rifles, Shotguns, Launchers, Service Rifles, Jump Packs.
* Lightning Damage is now Electric Damage.
* Thunder Damage is now Sonic Damage.
* Acid Damage is now Corrosion Damage.
* Currency is in the form of credits. Credits are a virtual currency and do not take up physical space.
* The moon of the planet changes phase each day.
* All playable races are made humanoids if they are not already.
* Targeting requires line of sight unless otherwise stated.
